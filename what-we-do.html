What We Do

Women Paying it Forward SA runs mentorship programmes.

The goal of the mentorship programme is to inspire, motivate and enhance self-esteem by exposing girls/ladies/learners to a Learning and Empowerment journey grounded on VALUES.

We all know when you educate a woman, you educate a nation.

The aim is to inspire them to make better-informed choices that will aid them in reaching their full potential.

How It's Done

The program is run by entering into a formal partnership with schools from historically disadvantaged communities where we host female learners by running quarterly workshops.

The girls/ladies/learners are paired with a mentor and the programme has quarterly themes based on inside-out leadership principles.

The themes covered are:

  • PERSONAL LEADERSHIP - Character
  • INTERPERSONAL LEADERSHIP – Connection in my immediate environment
  • PROFESSIONAL LEADERSHIP - Career and Community
  • PRACTICALS – Consolidation - Mentoring sessions - Evaluation and Graduation

About Us

There is a well-known African Proverb that says I AM BECAUSE WE ARE, and this has been an integral part of history and culture.

#WPIF_SA was birthed from this concept and the realization of the importance of paying it forward to ensure that others too can benefit from this concept that has helped shaped the lives of so many people.
