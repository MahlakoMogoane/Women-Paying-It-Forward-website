Our Mission

To create a platform for women to mentor young women about matters impacting our lives in society, through meaningful conversations. The intent is to empower young women to make better informed decisions whilst creating a culture of paying back to society. Make an Impact… Make a Difference… Be /act as a role model for the girl child and offer guidance to young people.

About Us

There is a well-known African Proverb that says I AM BECAUSE WE ARE, and this has been an integral part of history and culture.

#WPIF_SA was birthed from this concept and the realization of the importance of paying it forward to ensure that others too can benefit from this concept that has helped shaped the lives of so many people.
