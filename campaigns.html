Our Campaigns

Key Components of our programme include:

Monthly Personal Leadership Workshops

These interactive sessions equip participants with essential life skills, fostering self-awareness, goal-setting, and responsible decision-making.

Educational and Career Exposure Field Trips

We organise regular excursions to diverse environments—corporate offices, universities, cultural institutions, and more—to broaden participants’ horizons and inspire ambition.

Access to Further Education Opportunities

We provide guidance and access to additional educational pathways and opportunities, helping participants achieve their academic and professional goals.

Collaborative Support Networks

We partner with organisations that offer complementary services and interventions, enhancing the overall impact of our programme and contributing to the holistic upliftment of the youth we serve.

Comfort Closet Campaign

The Comfort Closet Campaign is a heartfelt initiative by #WPIF_SA aimed at restoring dignity and confidence to underprivileged learners. Launched on Mandela Day in 2021, the campaign partners with schools to establish a discreet, safe space where learners can access essential hygiene products.

Our goal is to ensure that no learner is held back by a lack of necessities. The Comfort Closet provides items such as:

  • Sanitary towels
  • Deodorant
  • Toothbrushes and toothpaste
  • Soap and other hygiene essentials

All items are distributed confidentially, ensuring learners can access what they need without fear of shame or discrimination.
