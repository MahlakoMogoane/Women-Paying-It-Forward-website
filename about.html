About Women Paying It Forward

Our Vision and Mission

To create a platform for women to mentor young women about matters impacting our lives in society, through meaningful conversations.

The intent is to empower young women to make better informed decisions whilst creating a culture of paying back to society.

Be and act as a role model for the girl child and offer guidance to young people.

Our Team

She is an HR professional with a BA Social Science qualification with approximately 20 years of extensive experience in different spheres of HR. She currently holds the position of Human Capital Executive at Motus Car Rental division.

In her personal capacity as well as in her HR journey she became extremely passionate about mentorship and has been involved in various mentorship platforms. The latter motivated her to start Women Paying It Forward SA (WPIFSA) in 2019.

She says: "Our objective is to enhance the skills of young people, particularly young girls in disadvantaged communities, and encourage them to achieve whatever goals they set out in life. I truly believe in the role of mentors and there is a need for such in our communities.

I value learning and enjoy genuine connections and engaging individuals from all walks of life, sharing people's passions and assisting them to achieve their aspirations. I am also a huge believer in the law of attraction and enjoy reading, with an interest in self-enhancement literature and I enjoy inspirational podcasts.

My dream for WPIF_SA is touch to the lives of our youth and act as a catalvst for them to become better versions of themselves, rise above their circumstances, contribute positively to society, and continue paying it forward.

One of my favourite quotes that I adopted from my late dad is that I am a firm believer in is "Dreamers are achievers"
